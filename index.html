In this school project, I implemented and compared several advanced association rule mining algorithms, including the Apriori algorithm, PCY algorithm, Random Sampling, SON algorithm, and the multihash version of the PCY algorithm. The primary objective was to uncover meaningful associations and frequent itemsets within massive datasets, and to assess the performance and effectiveness of each algorithm in this context. I conducted thorough experiments, analyzing various factors such as runtime efficiency, memory utilization, and the accuracy of association rule discovery. To evaluate the algorithms, I utilized two diverse datasets and explored different sample percentages (20%, 40%, 60%, 80%, and 100%) as well as support thresholds (1%, 2%, and 5%). I also paid specific attention to the number of false positives for Random Sampling, shedding light on its performance in the association rule mining domain.

To learn more about this project, please visit the github respository!

Dota 2 Scrim Tracker is a personal project that leverages Google Sheets to enable easy data entry, storage, and analysis of Dota 2 scrimmage matches. Dota 2 is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game known for its competitive gameplay and strategic team-based matches.

Using the Dota 2 Scrim Tracker in Google Sheets, you can effortlessly enter essential match details such as team names, match ids, side, pick priority, heroes, and other relevant information.

The project also offers basic statistical analysis using simple functions available in Google Sheets. By utilizing functions like "if," "concatenate," "countifs," "divide," and "sum," you can calculate essential statistics to gain insights into your team's performance.

Additionally, there is filtering that enable you to sort and retrieve specific scrimmage data based on team names, match ids, side, pick priority, heroes, and other relevant information. This feature simplifies the process of tracking performance, identifying trends, and analyzing team dynamics within your scrimmage matches.
